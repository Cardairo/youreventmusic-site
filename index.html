About the artist

Music that moves people.

Based out of Moysic Music & Creative Studio, I've spent over a decade performing at events that matter — weddings, memorials, galas, and everything in between. My approach is rooted in listening: to the room, to the moment, and to what you need.

Whether it's a quiet piano piece during a candle-lit ceremony or a full band set that gets the whole room dancing, every performance is crafted with intention and care.

  • Classically trained, versatile across genres
  • Experienced with sound systems and acoustic spaces
  • Collaborative — happy to learn your special song
  • Reliable, professional, and easy to work with
