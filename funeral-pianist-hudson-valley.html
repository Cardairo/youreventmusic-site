Common questions

Frequently asked.

How far in advance do I need to book?

We recommend reaching out as soon as you know the date, but we understand that funerals are often planned on short timelines. Contact us and we'll do everything we can to accommodate your needs.

Can you play specific songs or hymns?

Yes. We take song requests and will learn pieces that are meaningful to your family. Whether it's a traditional hymn, a favorite song, or a classical piece, just let us know when you inquire.

Do you work with funeral homes directly?

Absolutely. Funeral directors are welcome to contact us on behalf of families. We're experienced in coordinating directly with funeral home staff to ensure everything runs smoothly.

What areas do you serve?

We serve Hudson Valley, Newburgh, NYC, and surrounding New York areas. If you're unsure whether we cover your location, just reach out and ask.

How much does it cost?

Services typically start from $200 for solo piano. The final price depends on event length, location, and any special requests. We provide a free, no-obligation quote — just fill out the form below.

Do you play organ as well as piano?

Yes. We are experienced on both piano and organ, making us a great fit for churches and chapels with either instrument available.

Ready to arrange music for your service?

Fill out a quick inquiry and we'll get back to you within a few hours to confirm availability and discuss your needs.
